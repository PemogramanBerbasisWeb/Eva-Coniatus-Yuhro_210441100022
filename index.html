    In general, the meaning of writing is to express ideas, ideas and opinions in an article. Although writing is considered an activity that drains concentration, it turns out that there are also many who want to become writers. Indeed, being a writer is not just having intentions and ideals. But you must first understand the meaning of writing. Its function is to be able to deepen and be able to concentrate again in expressing ideas and ideas. After all, the desire to write has a great degree of utility. Not only beneficial for the author himself, but also beneficial for the reader himself. Back to focus on talking about the meaning of writing, there will be many variations of its meaning. Of course, from one opinion to another opinion is different. Therefore, on this occasion I will discuss some meanings of writing from the perspective of experts. Immediately, see the following reviews.

    1

    Writing Purpose

    Provide information about something, either in the form of facts, events, opinions, views and data to the reader. So that readers can get new insights and knowledge from the writing.

    2

    Write Function

    As an indirect means of communication between the writer and the reader. Because, in principle, writing can convey the author's message to the reader.

    3

    Benefits of writing

    This activity can also help generate new ideas, train the ability to build ideas, train an objective attitude towards others, help themselves to solve problems and encourage someone to be more active in seeking information.
